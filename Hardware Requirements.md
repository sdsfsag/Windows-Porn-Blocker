## How to Detect Overload
- **High CPU/GPU Usage** (90-100% load)
- **Lags or Slow Performance**
- **Overheating & Throttling**
- **Crashes or Freezes**

## Recommendation
AI functions should include a **performance check** to warn users with weak systems.
